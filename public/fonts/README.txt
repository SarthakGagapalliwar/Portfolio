Place your font files in the following folders to match src/app/fonts.css:
- public/fonts/rader/*.ttf
- public/fonts/messina-sans/*.otf
- public/fonts/messina-sans-mono/*.otf

Examples referenced in fonts.css:
- /fonts/rader/PPRader-Bold.ttf
- /fonts/messina-sans/MessinaSans-SemiBold.otf
- /fonts/messina-sans/MessinaSans-Regular.otf
- /fonts/messina-sans-mono/MessinaSansMono-SemiBold.otf
